History of Tapola

Tapola, known as "Mini Kashmir," began its journey as a tourist destination in the late 1980s through the efforts of Dr. Mahendra Kulkarni, who promoted eco-friendly tourism. The establishment of a tourism center in the 1990s, featuring gardens and cottages, marked the area's development. By the early 2000s, increased visitor interest led to better facilities, making Tapola a favorite weekend gateway for trekking and boating enthusiasts while emphasizing environmental protection.

Must-See Attractions

  • Shivsagar Lake with breathtaking views
  • Exciting water sports: swimming, boating, kayaking
  • Historic forts: Vasota and Jaygad
  • Adventurous jungle treks: Vasota Fort trek
  • Explore beautiful nameless islands
  • Kaas Plateau, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
